Graph-Augmented Agentic AIOps with Trust-Governed Digital Twins for Autonomous Cloud Workload Intelligence

Brij Mohan  ·  Uday Kiran Chilakalapalli  ·  Financial Services Industry
Read Paper → Google Scholar OrcID
Abstract

This paper introduces GA³Ops (Graph-Augmented Agentic AIOps), a framework leveraging trust-governed digital twins for autonomous cloud workload intelligence. The system integrates graph neural networks, temporal reasoning, and multi-agent reinforcement learning to enable self-healing, adaptive cloud operations with quantifiable trust guarantees.
